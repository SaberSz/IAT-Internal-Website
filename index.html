Automation Ideology

Our Intelligent Automation ideology revolves around our strategy of ‘Powering the Breakaway enterprise’ by aligning our solutions / services / operating models with its 4 pillars namely –

  1. Operate, to Transform (O2T): Digital workplace and business process transformation.
  2. Data-driven Organization: Digital / Virtual Assistants, Data led Automation (Data intake, Unstructured / semi-structured data extraction, Data aggregation & reporting automation).
  3. Experience Transformation: Persona-driven automation of business functions.
  4. Digitizing the core: Automation of horizontal & vertical business functions.

Core Competency

Process Automation

Automate mundane, repetitive, laborious tasks to minimize costs, increase efficiency, improve quality, transform UX and streamline business processes.

Intelligent Document Processing

Acquire and extract relevant data from structured and unstructured documents using cognitive data processing technologies combined with OCR.

Conversational AI

Combine both science and art to create intelligent digital assistants that incorporate context and personalization in a human to computer interaction.





Task & Process Mining

Data driven approach to gain insights into organizational processes and operations to standardize and optimize them.
