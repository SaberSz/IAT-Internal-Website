Banking & Finance Services

The Banking & Financial Services sector has been at the forefront of adopting Intelligent Automation (IA) and has also reaped multiple benefits such as cost optimization, process efficiency and revenue growth thanks to exploring newer ways of adopting and implementing digital transformation. IA has enabled the BFS industry to provide a superior customer experience while minimizing Cost to Income ratio.

LTI’s Intelligent Automation practice is actively engaged with leading BFS organizations for achieving their game plan of accelerated process transformation, coupled with gaining increased RoI. By leveraging different digital technologies such as Robotics Process Automation (RPA), Optical Character Recognition (OCR), Chatbots, workflow orchestration, Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning and other Cognitive technologies, LTI’s Intelligent Automation practice helps deliver end-to-end automation across the core areas of banking as well as the support functions viz. Human Resources, Finance, IT et al. The resulting digital workforce can be counted on to automate processes involving data extraction & consolidation, application integration and other manual rule-based repetitive processes quickly and without any errors, thereby freeing up the employees to work on better, strategic roles.

Our in-depth domain expertise augmented with exceptional delivery capability, enables us to make a notable difference across different areas such as Retail, Commercial, Private & Investment Banking, Wealth management and Capital Markets by automating E2E processes across Front, Middle & back office, including but not limited to Account On-boarding & KYC, Loan origination and approvals, Customer Service, Account Maintenance, Regulatory & Management Reporting, Reconciliation, Payment processing, Transaction processing and much more. Some of our banking focused solutions include Digital KYC Agent, Virtual Loan Assistant, SBA PPP Loans Processing Solution, Intelligent Invoice Processing Solution.
